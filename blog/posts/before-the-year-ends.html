before the year ends

28 December 2024

2024 was hard, i don't want to dress it up

but it was also the year i shipped something, started writing here, got more consistent with code, understood what burnout actually is and what to do about it, had some honest conversations with myself about what i want versus what i've been avoiding

i still don't have it figured out, i'm not sure the goal is to figure it out so much as to stop expecting it to already be figured out by now, i'm twenty five, i think i gave myself a deadline that was always unrealistic

next year i want to write more, build more things that are small and real, actually call my friends instead of just meaning to, keep the thirty minute habit going even when it feels pointless

that's enough, see you in january
