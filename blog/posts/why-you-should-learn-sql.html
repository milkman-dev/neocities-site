why you should learn sql already

28 February 2024

if you work anywhere near data or software and you don't know sql you are making your job harder than it needs to be, i'm not trying to be harsh, i just wish someone had told me this earlier

i picked it up kind of by accident, we had a data team and i kept having to ask them for things i needed, reports, queries, numbers for a deck, at some point i got tired of waiting and just started learning it myself, took me maybe two weekends to get to a level where i could do the basics

sql is not glamorous, nobody puts it in their twitter bio, but it's probably the most immediately useful thing i've learned in the past few years, you can see what's actually in the database, you can answer your own questions, you stop guessing and start knowing

the thing that surprised me is that sql changes how you think about problems in general, you stop thinking about one record at a time and start thinking about sets of data and the relationships between them, that shift is useful everywhere not just in a query window

just learn it, it's not that complicated and the return is immediate, you'll use it more than you expect
